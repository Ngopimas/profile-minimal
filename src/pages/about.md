I'm Romain Coupey, a software engineer based in Paris, France. I currently work at [Omaha Insights](https://www.omaha-insights.com/), where I build web applications that help businesses make sense of their data.

I started this blog as a way to document what I learn along the way — mostly about web development, cloud architecture, data visualization, and the occasional experiment with AI. Writing forces me to understand things deeply, and if it helps someone else stumble upon a solution, that's a nice bonus.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new tools, tinkering with side projects, and trying to keep up with how fast this industry moves.

If you want to get in touch, feel free to [send me an email](mailto:contact@romaincoupey.com) or reach out on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/romain-coupey/).
